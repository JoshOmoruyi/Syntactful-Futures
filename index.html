A little background about me....

I am currently a senior computer science student at my University

While I can say that my efforts and practices have brought me to a good standing, I can say that the ride was not a smooth one.

Before college, I was only exposed to computer science one time, yes, one time.

I was lucky to be able to register for a Mobile Csp Course at my high school my freshman year. This course was my true introduction to computer science and inspired me to continue to pursue it as a major. Unfortunately, this course was later removed as the only available professor had other obligations that conflicted with our school, and a replacement was never sought out, So just like that, a candle was blown out.

My first semester as a Compututer Science student was rough. I felt a constant overwelmness and my confidence lacked, while my peers who came from different areas were able to work through problems diligently.

It took me a while to understand and grasp concepts, but after getting the opportunity to get to meet some of my classmates, I came to a realization that my high school couldve better prepared us for STEM majors such as Computer Science.

Fast forward to 2025, a quick browse of my high school's website informs me that there is now a computer science department listed under STEM.

I am glad that local schooling is is finally taking the steps to prepare young students for the next level so they can feel confident, and that is what i intend to do with this.
